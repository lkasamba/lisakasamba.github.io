Brief Biography

Lisa Kasamba is a master's student at the UC Berkeley School of Information, focusing on product management and data science. She is passionate about finding ingenious ways to bring about efficacy and efficiency. Lisa was born and raised in the Kingdom of ESwatini and she graduated from Methodist University, in Fayetteville, North Carolina with a double major in applied forensic science and justice studies. In her free time, she enjoys traveling, trying new foods, and learning new languages.
